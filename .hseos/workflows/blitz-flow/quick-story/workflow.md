# Quick Story

## Intent
Run a compressed single-story flow for small changes that do not require the full epic pipeline.

## Owner
BLITZ

## Phases
1. Capture scope and acceptance criteria.
2. Check whether ADR or broader planning is required.
3. Implement, test, and validate in one session.
4. Record concise evidence.

## Gates
- Stop and split when the task exceeds one session or crosses architecture boundaries.
