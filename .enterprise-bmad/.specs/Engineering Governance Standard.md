# Engineering Governance Standard

**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Purpose
Defines how technical decisions are made, enforced and evolved.

## 2. Decision Authority
- Architecture Standards are mandatory
- Deviations require ADR approval

## 3. ADR Policy
- ADR required for:
  - Architectural changes
  - Breaking changes
  - Cross-context coupling

## 4. Enforcement
- Standards are enforced via PR review and CI gates
- Non-compliance blocks merge

## 5. Evolution of Standards
- Standards are versioned
- Changes require review and communication

## Summary
Engineering decisions are explicit, documented and enforceable.
